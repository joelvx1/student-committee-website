What we do

The Staff/Student Liaison Committee (SSLCom) was created to create a bridge between students and staff. We take matters such as teaching, examinations, departmental polices and student issues and raise this to the department to fix problems and make our University better. Our Student Reps can raise the views of students to the department regarding: student feedback on the quality of teaching; inputs to the planning of curriculum changes; departmental services (e.g. hand-in arrangements, office opening times, study facilities, the departmental network, availability of personal tutors etc.); improving channels of communication between staff and students students.
